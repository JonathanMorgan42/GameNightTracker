How Point Schemes Work

Formula:
Points = (Teams - Place + 1) × Point Scheme

Example with 4 teams, {{ game.point_scheme }}× multiplier:

  • 1st place: (4 - 1 + 1) × {{ game.point_scheme }} = {{ 4 * game.point_scheme }} points
  • 2nd place: (4 - 2 + 1) × {{ game.point_scheme }} = {{ 3 * game.point_scheme }} points
  • 3rd place: (4 - 3 + 1) × {{ game.point_scheme }} = {{ 2 * game.point_scheme }} points
  • 4th place: (4 - 4 + 1) × {{ game.point_scheme }} = {{ 1 * game.point_scheme }} point

Higher multipliers mean more points for each position!

Point Calculation Formula

Points = (Number of Teams - Rank + 1) × Point Multiplier

Example: With 6 teams and a ×2 multiplier:

  • 1st place: (6 - 1 + 1) × 2 = 12 points
  • 3rd place: (6 - 3 + 1) × 2 = 8 points
  • 6th place: (6 - 6 + 1) × 2 = 2 points

Penalties

Stackable Penalties: Can be applied multiple times (e.g., +5 sec each time a rule is broken)

One-time Penalties: Apply once per team (e.g., +10 sec for failing a specific challenge)

For time-based games: Penalties are added to your time (higher = worse)
For point-based games: Penalties are subtracted from your score (lower score = worse)
